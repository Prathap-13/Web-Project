             Indian film director, screenwriter Prashanth Neel. He was born on 4 June 1980, in Hassan, Karnataka, India. Prashanth Neel is an Indian film director and screenwriter who primarily works in the Kannada film industry. He gained widespread recognition and acclaim for his debut directorial venture, "KGF: Chapter 1," released in 2018. The film became a massive hit and garne red attention not only in Karnataka but also across the country.
